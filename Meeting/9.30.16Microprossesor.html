What is a microprocessor
an integrated circuit that contains all the functions of a central processing unit of a computer.
A litte city with skyscrapers, where circuits go over and under one another.
A transistor is a semiconductor device used to amplify or switch electronic signals and electrical power. It is composed of semiconductor material usually with at least three terminals for connection to an external circuit.

1. List the four steps in the computer chip design process
1. architectural design stage- designs features (ex- how tall? elevators? etc)
2. Logic design- builers who use what the architects design to put blocks together
3. Circuit design- build physical circuits to connect transistors
4. Mask design- run simulations
2. Explain the extreme environment necessary to make computer chips
Clean rooms (1000 times cleaner than hospital surgical room!), 40 steps to suit up, Even breath can contaminate chips because the chips are so small
3. Describe how masks are used to create each layer of computer chips

1. lay down a material- either a conductor or insulator
2. Add photo resist chemical
3. Add mask that contains images of the circuits
4. Expose it to light, which exposes the photoresist
5. Etch the photoresist away to get the pattern needed
They have to carve the pattern out instead depositing it onto the wafer
6. Add chemicals to get the properties they want
