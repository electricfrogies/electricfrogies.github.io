What is a Robot anyway?

1. List examples of robots (real and fictional)
Wall-e, Humanoids, satellites, GPS, Computers, Mars rover, R2D2, C3PO
2. State characteristics of a robot
Follows instructions, Has computer programming, Can sense its environment, It is manufactured
3. Describe examples of how robots sense their environment
The robot the can sense the wall, light sensors
4. Describe examples of how robots follow instructions
Robots could stop when they are near a wall. We program them to do this.
